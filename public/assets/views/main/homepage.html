Our Success Stories

Carter was alone when they first started producing music in their tree house, but one magical day they stumbled upon our website while misspelling 'muse'. Now they have 3 studio albums and are the founder of the country-vapor genre of music, a genre where water meets a cowboy.
Lola is known for her animosity in her lyrics and sound. She has remained faceless throughout her decade long career but signed to Your Mus(ic} records 1 year ago when she realized she could keep her sound while reaching a whole new audience. Though you can’t see her face, she is happy with her choice.
I am Bob and this Bobcat. We are music nerds who found each other on a shipwreck and haven’t separated since. Bobcat has a melodic ‘meowing’ voice that other record labels didn’t understand. I needed a community that truly listened to our music and was able to convey connection across the internet. I found Your Mus(ic) Records perfectly encapsulates our vibes while adding new sounds. Now I never have to leave my accordion behind. Bobcat agrees “meow”.
We are Gurlbee, a pop/rock band that dabbles in mariachi. Some may consider us industry plants even though we had humble beginnings. With liars and posers knocking on your door every second in this industry, Your Mus(ic) Records has proved themselves to be an authentic record label dedicated to the passions of their artists’. We would have never been able to be nominated for 3 Grammy’s last year if it wasn’t for them.
This is Rory and Homer. Before Your Mus(ic) Records were just a couple who fought all the time, now they are a pop duo that still fight all the time. One day Rory was on our website listening to our sample sounds page when Homer came bursting in, accusing her of stealing his monocle and mustache. She left the sound on and they started arguing, the rest is history. They are currently working on their second album “Monocle & Stache” available in January 2025.
